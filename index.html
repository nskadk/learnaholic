I posted a session goal, but it doesn't acknowledge my session goal. I am in the screen/cam study channel.

#auto-accountability doesn't apply to the screen/cam study rooms. Why? In screen/cam study channels, you are required to either screen share or cam, ensuring that you are indeed studying (no need to check up on you every 2 hours: we already know you're working).

Should you have any questions after joining, feel free to drop us an email at help@studytogether.com or write one of the moderators in the chat

How long does the timer run & how long is the break?

You can use our study timer to help you organize your own study session. Each learning session runs 50 minutes and is followed by a 10 minutes break. This way you have a good help to get more done but you do not have to follow the timer if you do not feel like it of course

Absolutely. You are able to join the call without a working camera - either way, we will be there with you
